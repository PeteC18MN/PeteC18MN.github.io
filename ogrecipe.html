Sherbet Shrub:

History of The Drink:

Shrub is a non-alchoholic beverage drank during Thanksgiving. It's composed of many
fruit juices, ginger ale, and sherbet put on the top. It's most typically used with alcohol,
however, not in this recipe. It's history began in the 15th century, as people would smuggle,
and due to its long lasting concentration, it became very popular. Later in America, it became
popular as being infused with both a soft drink or beverage. Though, it declined in popularity due to
home refrigiration, it's still served in many American and Canadian restauraunts.
